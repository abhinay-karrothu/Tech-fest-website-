About Advika

Welcome to the technical fest ADVIKA 2K18 in the campus of Adikavi Nannaya Unviersity. We are pleased and Excited to invite you all for the three day fest to be held on .On this occasion we Challenge your Ability and Knowledge over Technology and other Aspects with a mixture of your talents.It is a Platform to Show your Talent and Ideas to many others.So, Dont Let Go Away the Oppurtunity for many Events and Surprises are waiting for You.This Event is conducted by the College of Engineering, Adikavi Nannaya University, Rajamahendravaram.

The immense intention of organising the ADVIKA 2K18 is to recieve naive ideas of the students by providing a platform to exibit them and get awareness about the current technologies on 'how' principle rather than 'what'. To realize their stand of point in that aspect and improve their skills. ADVIKA aims at the student fraternity to utilize the opportunity to enhance/exibit their abilities and induce a different expirience.We Think You'll have fun!!!
