About University

Adikavi Nannaya University was established on 22nd April,2006 by the state Government of Andhra Pradesh to promote higher education in the Godavari Region.The University  has 486 affliated colleges including Under Education,Post Graduation & Education. The University under the professional/academic gudiance of Hon’ble Vice Chancellor  Prof.Murru Mutyalu Naidu’s presently stridling to imprint ‘a mark of excellence’.In addi tion a number of colleges being offered presently in the university colleges of Arts &  Commerce, Science & Technology, Engineering & Education the university is visualizing to initiate a few new Post Graduation courses from the academic year 2016-17.

About University College of Engineering

University College Of Engineering was established since the inception of the University. It offers two post graduate programes in M.Tech(CST), MCA, and four under graduate programes B.tech(CSE,EIE,INF,ECE).University College of Engineering also offers Ph.D Program. University College of Engineering Excels with the potential of excellent Faculty with diverse research areas.The Objective of University College of Engineering is to achieve excellence in various fields of the technology and to be with in the vicinity of Industry demands combined with reasearch oriented thoughts. Students are progressively exposed to a gamut of subjects from the core related to advanced technologies which will make them competent enough in the competitive era in the fields of the Computer Science and Information & Communication Technology.
